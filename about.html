More About Me

Hello, my name is Ben Foster. I am an Information Technology Asssociate located in New York City. I recently left the US Army in pursuit of a career in IT. My passion for security has driven me towards earning my CompTIA Security+ certification as a launching point for my career. While I mainly work in Network Administration now, I enjoy learning about network and security engineering, risk mitigation, disaster response, and software development.

I'm originally from Eau Claire, Wisconsin, but I moved out to New York to attend the United States Military Academy at West Point. After graduating with a BS in Organizational Psychology and Leadership, I commissioned as a Second Lieutenant in the Army and soon became a Platoon Leader.
