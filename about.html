More About Me

Hello, my name is Ben Foster. I am an Information Technology Asssociate located in New York City. I recently left the US Army in pursuit of a career in IT. My passion for security has driven me towards earning my CompTIA Security+ certification as a launching point for my career. While I mainly work in Network Administration now, I enjoy learning about network and security engineering, risk mitigation, disaster response, and software development.

I'm originally from Eau Claire, Wisconsin, but I moved out to New York to attend the United States Military Academy at West Point. After graduating with a BS in Organizational Psychology and Leadership, I commissioned as a Second Lieutenant in the Army and soon became a Platoon Leader. Although my Army career came to a quicker close that I would've liked I am very grateful for the experiences I had and the people I met While I was in. I hope to take the lessons I learned about professional development, leadership, and character into the private sector as I grow in my IT skills and improve the organizations I find myself a part of.

However, as much as my professional pursuits are governed by the effort I put into the process, my motivation comes in greatest part from my small family with my wife Jill and our dog Winston! Without them, I would not have the drive for success that I do and the support to make it all happen. I love them dearly and I get confidence in my daily life from their love and God's love.
